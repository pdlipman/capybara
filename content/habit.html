Habitat

Region

Capybaras are found throughout almost all countries of South America except Chile.

Home

They live in densely forested areas near bodies of water, such as lakes, rivers, swamps, ponds, and marshes, as well as flooded savannah and along rivers in tropical forest.

Habitat Requirements

Capybara have flourished in cattle ranches. They roam in home ranges averaging 10 hectares (25 acres) in high-density populations.
